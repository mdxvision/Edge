## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Creating an Account

**Step 1: Navigate to the Login Page**
- Open your browser and go to the EdgeBet application URL
- You will see the welcome screen with "Welcome to EdgeBet" and "Intelligent edge detection. Beautifully simple."

**Step 2: Select Registration Mode**
- Look for two tabs at the top of the form: **"Get Started"** and **"Sign In"**
- Click **"Get Started"** (this should be selected by default)

**Step 3: Enter Your Email**
- Locate the email input field
- Enter a valid email address (e.g., `yourname@example.com`)
- This will be used for account recovery and notifications

**Step 4: Choose a Username**
- Find the field labeled **"Choose a username"**
- Enter a unique username (this will be visible on leaderboards)
- Usernames must be unique across the platform

**Step 5: Create a Password**
- Locate the password field labeled **"At least 8 characters"**
- Enter a strong password with:
  - Minimum 8 characters
  - Recommended: mix of letters, numbers, and symbols

**Step 6: Confirm Your Password**
- Find the field labeled **"Confirm your password"**
- Re-enter the exact same password
- If passwords don't match, you'll see an error: "Passwords don't match"

**Step 7: Set Your Starting Bankroll**
- Locate the bankroll input field
- Enter your starting virtual bankroll amount
- Minimum: $100
- This is used to calculate stake recommendations

**Step 8: Select Your Risk Profile**
- Find the dropdown menu for risk profile
- Choose one of three options:
  - **Conservative**: Lower stakes, typically 0.5% of bankroll per bet
  - **Balanced**: Moderate risk, typically 1% of bankroll per bet
  - **Aggressive**: Higher stakes, typically 2% of bankroll per bet

**Step 9: Age Verification**
- Locate the checkbox at the bottom of the form
- Check the box confirming you are **21 years or older**
- This is required to create an account

**Step 10: Submit Registration**
- Click the **"Create Account"** or **"Continue"** button
- Wait for the system to process your registration
- Upon success, you will be redirected to the Dashboard

**Troubleshooting:**
- "Email already exists" → Use a different email or try logging in
- "Username taken" → Choose a different username
- "Password needs at least 8 characters" → Make your password longer

### 1.2 Logging In

**Step 1: Navigate to Login Page**
- Go to the EdgeBet application URL
- You will see the welcome screen

**Step 2: Select Sign In Mode**
- Click the **"Sign In"** tab at the top of the form

**Step 3: Enter Your Credentials**
- **Email field** (placeholder: "you@example.com"): Enter your registered email
- **Password field** (placeholder: "Your password"): Enter your password

**Step 4: Submit Login**
- Click the **"Continue"** button
- Wait for authentication to complete

**Step 5: Two-Factor Authentication (if enabled)**
- If you have 2FA enabled, you'll see a code input field
- Open your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
- Enter the 6-digit code displayed in your app
- Click **"Verify"** or **"Continue"**

**Step 6: Access Dashboard**
- Upon successful login, you'll be redirected to your Dashboard
- You should see your username displayed in the sidebar

**Quick Login (Development Only):**
- If available, click the **"Dev Login"** button to bypass credentials
- This is only available in development/testing environments

### 1.3 Password Recovery

**Step 1: Access Forgot Password**
- On the Sign In form, click **"Forgot password?"** link

**Step 2: Enter Your Email**
- Enter the email address associated with your account
- Click **"Send Reset Link"**

**Step 3: Check Your Email**
- Open your email inbox
- Look for an email from EdgeBet with subject "Password Reset"
- Click the reset link in the email

**Step 4: Create New Password**
- Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters)
- Confirm the new password
- Click **"Reset Password"**

**Step 5: Login with New Password**
- Return to the login page
- Enter your email and new password
- Click **"Continue"**

### 2.2 Setting Your Risk Profile

**Understanding Risk Profiles:**

| Profile | Typical Stake | Best For |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| Conservative | 0.5% of bankroll | New users, capital preservation |
| Balanced | 1% of bankroll | Most users, moderate growth |
| Aggressive | 2% of bankroll | Experienced users, higher risk tolerance |

**Step 1: Navigate to Profile Page**
- Click **"Profile"** in the sidebar

**Step 2: Locate Risk Profile Section**
- Scroll to the **"Risk Profile"** section
- You'll see three radio button options

**Step 3: Select Your Risk Level**
- Click on your preferred option:
  - **Conservative**: "Lower stakes, 0.5% of bankroll typical. Best for preserving capital."
  - **Balanced**: "Moderate risk, 1% of bankroll typical. Good balance of growth and safety."
  - **Aggressive**: "Higher stakes, 2% of bankroll typical. Maximum growth potential."

**Step 4: Save Changes**
- Click **"Save Changes"** at the bottom
- Your stake recommendations will now reflect this setting

**Impact on Recommendations:**
- When you view picks, the "Suggested Stake" will be calculated based on:
  - Your bankroll amount
  - Your risk profile percentage

## 3. Daily Workflow

### 3.1 Dashboard Overview

**Accessing the Dashboard:**
- After login, you land on the Dashboard automatically
- Or click **"Today"** in the sidebar under "Betting" section

**Understanding Dashboard Sections:**

**Top Section - Greeting & Stats Cards:**
- Personalized greeting based on time of day
- Four stat cards showing:
  1. **Bankroll**: Your current virtual bankroll amount
  2. **Curated Picks**: Number of AI-recommended picks available
  3. **Precision Rate**: Average edge percentage across picks
  4. **Projected Edge**: Expected value in dollars

**Middle Section - Quick Picks:**
- Shows top 5 curated picks at a glance
- Each pick displays:
  - Sport badge (NFL, NBA, MLB, etc.)
  - Teams/matchup
  - Selection and odds
  - Edge percentage (green = positive, red = negative)
  - Suggested stake
- Click **"See All"** to view full recommendations

**Bottom Section - Upcoming Games:**
- Shows next 5 games across all sports
- Displays:
  - Sport badge
  - Team names
  - Game time
  - Venue
- Click **"See All"** to browse all games

**Daily Workflow from Dashboard:**
1. Check your stats cards for overall status
2. Review Quick Picks for immediate opportunities
3. Click through to Recommendations for detailed analysis
4. Browse Upcoming Games for research

### 3.2 Browsing Games

**Step 1: Navigate to Games**
- Click **"Matchups"** in the sidebar under "Betting"
- Or click **"See All"** from Dashboard's Upcoming Games

**Step 2: Select a Sport**
- Look at the sport tabs at the top:

  - **CBB** (College Basketball)

**Step 3: View Game Information**
Each game card displays:

- **Time**: Game start time in your local timezone
- **Status**: Scheduled, In Progress, or Final
- **Venue**: Stadium/arena name
- **Broadcast**: TV channel (if national broadcast)

**Sport-Specific Information:**
- **MLB**: Weather conditions, wind speed/direction, innings
- **NBA**: Quarter, national TV indicator
- **NFL**: Quarter, national TV indicator
- **NHL**: Period, shots on goal
- **CBB**: Team rankings, conference

**Step 4: View Game Details**
- Click on any game card to open detailed view
- See additional information:
  - Full odds (spread, total, moneyline)
  - Team statistics
  - Head-to-head history
  - Weather impact (if applicable)

**Step 5: Log a Pick from Games**
- Click the **"Log Pick"** button on a game
- A modal will appear to track your bet
- Fill in your selection and stake

**Step 6: Refresh Data**
- Click the **"Refresh"** button to get latest scores/odds

### 3.3 Viewing Recommendations

**Step 1: Navigate to Recommendations**
- Click **"Picks"** in the sidebar under "Betting"
- Or click **"See All"** from Dashboard's Quick Picks

**Step 2: Generate Fresh Picks**
- Click the **"Generate Picks"** button at the top
- Wait for the AI to analyze current opportunities
- New picks will populate the list

**Step 3: Apply Filters**

**Sport Filter:**
- Click the sport dropdown (default: "All Sports")
- Select a specific sport to narrow results:
  - All Sports
  - NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL
  - CFB, CBB
  - Soccer

**Minimum Edge Filter:**
- Click the edge dropdown (default: "Any Edge")
- Select minimum edge threshold:
  - Any Edge (show all)
  - 2%+ (moderate edge)
  - 5%+ (strong edge)
  - 10%+ (exceptional edge)

**Step 4: Understanding Pick Cards**

Each recommendation shows:

**Header Section:**
- Sport badge (e.g., "NFL" in team color)
- Market type badge (e.g., "Spread", "Total", "Moneyline")
- Sportsbook badge (e.g., "DraftKings", "FanDuel")

**Game Information:**
- Teams: "Kansas City Chiefs vs Baltimore Ravens"
- Game time and date

**Selection Details:**
- **Selection**: The recommended bet (e.g., "Chiefs -3.5")
- **Odds**: American odds format (e.g., "-110")
- **Edge**: Percentage edge over the market (e.g., "+4.2%")
- **Confidence**: Model confidence level (e.g., "78%")
- **Suggested Stake**: Dollar amount based on your bankroll/risk profile

**Edge Explanation Box:**
- AI-generated rationale for the pick
- Example: "Chiefs have covered 8 of last 10 as favorites. Ravens 2-5 ATS on short rest."

**Edge Factor Badges:**
- **Strong Value**: Edge exceeds 5%
- **Line Tracked**: Line movement detected
- **Official Factor**: Referee/umpire analysis included
- **Rest Analyzed**: Rest days factored in
- **Travel Factor**: Travel distance considered

**Step 5: Acting on Recommendations**
- Review the edge explanation and confidence
- Check the suggested stake against your comfort level
- If you want to bet:
  - Go to Paper Trading to practice
  - Go to Tracking to log a real bet
  - Place the bet at your sportsbook

## 4. Betting Workflows

### 4.1 Paper Trading (Practice)

**Purpose:** Practice betting with virtual money to test strategies risk-free.

**Step 1: Navigate to Paper Trading**
- Click **"Paper Trading"** in the sidebar under "Practice"

**Step 2: View Your Virtual Stats**
- **Balance**: Your current virtual bankroll
- **Profit/Loss**: Total P/L since starting
- **ROI**: Return on investment percentage
- **Win Rate**: Percentage of winning bets

**Step 3: Place a Virtual Bet**

1. Click the **"Place Bet"** button
2. Fill out the bet form:
   - **Sport**: Select from dropdown (NFL, NBA, MLB, etc.)
   - **Bet Type**: Select type (Spread, Moneyline, Total, Prop)
   - **Selection**: Describe your pick (e.g., "Chiefs -3.5")
   - **Odds**: Enter American odds (e.g., "-110")
   - **Stake**: Enter amount to wager
   - **Line** (optional): Enter the line value
3. Click **"Place Bet"** to confirm

**Step 4: Manage Open Bets**
- View your pending bets in the **"Open Bets"** section
- Each bet shows:
  - Selection and sport
  - Stake and potential payout
  - Odds

**Step 5: Settle Bets**
- When a game completes, settle your bet:
  - Click **✓ (checkmark)** if the bet won
  - Click **✗ (X)** if the bet lost
  - Click **— (dash)** if the bet pushed (tie)

**Step 6: Analyze Performance**
- **Performance by Sport**: See W-L record and ROI per sport
- **Recent History**: View all settled bets with results
- **Stats Panel**: Track streaks, best/worst bets

**Step 7: Reset Bankroll (Optional)**
- Click **"Reset"** button to start fresh
- This clears all history and resets to initial bankroll
- Use this to test new strategies from scratch

---

### 4.2 Tracking Real Bets

**Purpose:** Log and track bets you place at real sportsbooks.

**Step 1: Navigate to Tracking**
- Click **"My Bets"** in the sidebar under "Betting"

**Step 2: View Performance Stats**
- **Performance**: Total profit/loss (green = profit, red = loss)
- **Precision Rate**: Win percentage
- **ROI**: Return on investment
- **Current Streak**: Consecutive wins or losses

**Step 3: Add a New Bet**

1. Click **"Track a Bet"** button
2. Complete the form:
   - **Sport**: Select the sport
   - **Bet Type**: Moneyline, Spread, Total, Prop, or Future
   - **Selection**: Describe your bet (e.g., "Lakers +4.5")
   - **Odds**: Enter the odds you got
   - **Stake**: Enter the amount wagered
   - **Sportsbook** (optional): Where you placed the bet
3. Click **"Track This"** to save

**Step 4: Filter Your Bets**

**By Status:**
- **All Status**: View all bets
- **In Play**: View unsettled bets
- **Settled**: View completed bets

**By Sport:**
- **All Sports**: View all
- Select specific sport to filter

**Step 5: Settle Bets**
- Find your pending bet in the list
- Click one of the settle buttons:
  - **"Cashed"**: Bet won
  - **"Missed"**: Bet lost
  - **"Push"**: Bet tied/refunded

**Step 6: Review Sidebar Statistics**

**Leaderboard Preview:**
- See top 10 users by profit
- Check your ranking

**Your Edge Stats:**
- Total bets placed
- Win-Loss record
- Total amount staked
- Average odds
- Best win / Worst loss
- Best winning streak

### 4.3 Building Parlays

**Purpose:** Analyze multi-leg parlays for correlation and expected value.

**Step 1: Navigate to Parlays**
- Click **"Parlays"** in the sidebar under "Betting"

**Step 2: Add Your First Pick**

1. In the **"Add Pick"** card:
   - **Selection**: Enter the pick (e.g., "Chiefs -3.5")
   - **Odds**: Enter American odds (e.g., "-110")
   - **Your Probability %**: Enter your estimated win probability (e.g., "55")
   - **Sport** (optional): Select the sport for better correlation detection
2. Click **"Add Pick"** button

**Step 3: Add Additional Picks**
- Repeat Step 2 for each leg of your parlay
- Minimum 2 picks required for analysis
- Each pick appears in the **"Your Picks"** list

**Step 4: Review Your Legs**
- Each leg displays:
  - Leg number
  - Selection text
  - Sport badge (if provided)
  - Odds
  - Your probability estimate
- Click **"×"** to remove a leg

**Step 5: Analyze the Parlay**
- Once you have 2+ picks, click **"Analyze Parlay"**
- Wait for the analysis to complete

**Step 6: Review Analysis Results**

**Main Metrics:**
- **Combined Odds**: Total parlay odds
- **Win Probability**: Adjusted probability accounting for correlation
- **Edge %**: Your edge vs the market
- **Projected Edge**: Expected value per dollar bet

**Correlation Warning:**
- If picks are correlated, you'll see a warning banner
- Shows correlation adjustment (boost or reduction)
- Example: "Correlation detected: -3.2% adjustment"

**EV Assessment:**
- **+EV Parlay** (green): Positive expected value
- **Negative EV** (red): Negative expected value

**Risk Assessment:**
- Risk level: Low / Medium / High
- Adjusted win probability
- Suggested max stake (as % of bankroll)
- Risk recommendation text

**Leg Breakdown:**
- Individual edge calculation for each pick

**Step 7: Make Your Decision**
- If +EV with acceptable risk: Consider placing the parlay
- If -EV or high correlation: Consider adjusting picks
- Use the suggested max stake as a guide

**Step 8: Clear and Start Over**
- Click **"Clear All"** to remove all picks
- Start building a new parlay
